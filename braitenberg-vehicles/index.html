Instructions:

  • Choose Connection mode: Direct or Crossed using the top toggle.
  • Select Strength: Inhibit or Excite using the second toggle.
  • Click Place Lights, then click anywhere on the canvas to add and right click to remove light sources (stimuli).
  • Click Place Vehicles, then click on the canvas to add vehicles, or right-click on a vehicle to remove it. Click the button again to stop placing vehicles.
  • Click Start Simulation to begin. You can pause/resume the simulation with the same button.
  • While simulation is running, controls are disabled to prevent changes.
